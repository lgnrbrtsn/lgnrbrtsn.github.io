Meeting Tuesday evenings starting 7pm MTN, October 10th through November 7th

Minister and advocate Kevin Nye introduces readers to the Christ he's met in tents, shelters, and drop-in centers. In an approachable way, Nye answers the basic questions and provides a bigger framework toward how people of faith can engage homelessness in their local community. Through a Christian lens, Nye presents the various best practices and challenges regarding ending homelessness, including housing first, structural racism, economic justice and many other approaches and considerations.

Backpack is a bookclub for curious people.

Current series: Jesus and the Disinherited by Howard Thurman.

Meeting Monday evenings 7pm, March 29—April 26

Thurman was one who made the paths straight for the African American Civil Rights Movement of the 1960s. A forerunner and patriarch to the men and women who would organize, struggle, and demand justice out of the legacy of slavery and the American Jim Crow South, Thurman offers an interpretation of the teachings of Jesus through the experience of the oppressed and offers nonviolent responses to oppression.

Mondays: March 29, April 5, 12, 19, 26 on Zoom
